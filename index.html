My Favorite Dinasour

The Argentinosaurus is my favorite dinosaur because it is one of the largest land animals of all time. It reached lengths of over 100 feet and weight estimates range from 50 to 100 tons. The large size of some dinasaurs has always been fascinating for me, with memories of seeing dinasaur bones during school field trips to the Field Museum in Chicago.

John Maple, 1/10/2021
